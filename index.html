Debora Nozza

Postdoctoral Research Fellow

Data and Marketing Insights research unit at Bocconi University

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Computer Science at the Bocconi Institute for Data Science and Analytics at Bocconi University in the Bocconi NLP group for the INTEGRATOR ERC Starting Grant project.

My research interests mainly focus on the detection and counter-acting of hate speech and bias which has become fundamental for fighting against several types of discrimination that subsist in Social Media. I organized several shared tasks for the problem of hate speech, and in particular misogyny detection (HatEval@SemEval 2019, AMI@Evalita 2020, AMI@Evalita 2018)
